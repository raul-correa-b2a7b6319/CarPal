How CarPal Works

How Do We Find A Driver / Passenger?

We connect drivers and passengers based on your location, extracurriculars, and your arrival and departure times from Mount Carmel to ensure the best match for your schedule and needs. Our smart matching system ensures drivers and passengers live within 5.5 miles of each other for optimal convenience and reliability.

Driver Information

License Duration

At least 4 months with a valid driver’s license.

You must have held your license for at least 4 months before applying.

Insurance Required

Your vehicle must be insured at all times.

Proof of insurance is required for all drivers.

Accident Limit

No more than 1 car accident on record.

Drivers with more than 1 accident are not eligible.

Safety Responsibility

Drivers are responsible for passenger safety.

You must agree to prioritize the safety and comfort of your passengers at all times.

Guardian Meeting

Virtual meeting with guardians and CarPal required.

A virtual meeting ensures everyone is comfortable and informed.

Professionalism

Maintain a clean record and clear communication.

Drivers must communicate clearly and uphold CarPal’s standards.

We are three Mount Carmel High School students ('24) who were frustrated by the high cost of transportation and the inconvenience of early bus schedules. Determined to find a better solution, we built CarPal, a student-driven ride-share service designed for our community.

Our innovative approach was recognized when we won a $2,500 pitch competition. We're committed to making safe, affordable, and reliable transportation accessible to all Mount Carmel students.
